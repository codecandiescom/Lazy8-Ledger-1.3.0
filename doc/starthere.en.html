Start here

This will help you get going as quickly as possible.  Follow the steps and you will soon be doing well.

1.  Installation.  This is really a pain in the neck for those who do not know how to program.  But follow my installation instructions and you will soon be doing fine.  If you have a CD with this program, then the installation process will be much more simple and you shouldnt even need to read anything about installation.  Strangely enough, if you are reading this, than you have probably already done the installation process....The first time you start Lazy8 Ledger, you should be prompted to enter a Company, Accounts, etc. If you didnt do this process, then go to the menu ADVANCED-CREATE A NEW COMPANY. After you do this, you can then proceed with step 5 below. If you want instead to do everything manually, do steps 2 to 4 first and then continue with step 5.
2.  After installation, Enter your company name.  see the menu DataEntry->Company.
3.  Enter the default company.  This will be the one you just entered in part 2.  see the menu Options->Setup.
4.  Enter your accounts.  see the menu DataEntry->Account. As an alternative to entering all your own accounts, you can use a predefined group of accounts that follow the EU BAS 2000 accounting system that comes with this program. These predefined accounts represent the most commonly used accounts for small businesses. To load up these predefined accounts, choose the menu Advanced->CREATE A NEW COMPANY.
5.  Enter your customers.  This is optional.  Most wont need to do this but if you want to have special statistics on your specific customers, then .  see the menu DataEntry->Customer.
6.  Enter your invoices.  See the menu DataEntry->Transaction.
7.  Print out all you have done.  This of course is optional but it is also really fun.  Go to the menu Report and have fun with all the reports.
8.  Backup what you have done.  This is optional but highly suggested. see backups
9.  Now you are an advanced user and you should feel free to browse all the other menus but please before pressing any buttons in those menus, check out the HELP information first.

Good luck and have lots of fun.....
